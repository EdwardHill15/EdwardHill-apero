Introduction

There’s a slight temperature rise measured in the Netherlands between 1901 and 2022. Though the maximum temperatures near 37 degrees of Celsius were also measured respectively 15, 30, 50 and 60 years ago.

Conclusion

My conclusion based on these data is that there is light positive trend of temperature rise from 1901 to 2022 but this doesn’t suppport the idea of a climate-crisis based on measured temperature differences.
